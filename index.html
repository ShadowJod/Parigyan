A Journey of Wisdom & Wellness

Parigyan Yogalaya, founded by Neha Pradeep Sangodkar, is dedicated to spreading the ancient wisdom of yoga through modern, accessible teaching. We believe in holistic well-being — body, mind, and spirit.
